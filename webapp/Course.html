Why Ph.D. ?

PhD is one of the most prestigious degrees that a student can pursue. With the recent focus by the government to encourage research education in the country, a PhD degree is considered the most viable option.

To gain a deeper insight into "Why to choose a PhD," below are a few points listed:

  • A PhD degree offers various career opportunities in research, academia, or other specialized areas of work.
  • There has been an increase of 13.7% in enrollment in PhD from the previous year.
  • The advancement of technology has made it easier for students to access research resources and collaborate with researchers from other countries.
  • The Indian government has taken several initiatives to support and promote PhD courses in India such as MERU, IMPRINT, Prime Minister Research Fellowship, Global Initiative of Academic Networks, etc.
